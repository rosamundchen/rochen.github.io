Stir Conference

The task was to create a "provocation" for a design conference using visual semiotics.

How does design affect the way we perceive information? Can our minds and emotions be manipulated through design? STIR Conference talks about the co-relation between design and psychology. It uses the manipulation of the cursor on the brain as its main identity.
