2019

Wellington Conversations


Wellington Conversations is a free event series that allows Wellingtonians to connect over facilitated conversations about topics that matter.

The task was to design a vibrant and approachable visual identity for all walks of life. The logotype is influenced by the peaks and troughs of soundwaves, as well as the Te Reo translation which centres around ‘take’, meaning the tree’s base and the natural world of growth around meaningful conversations.
